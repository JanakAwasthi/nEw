How to Use Photos to PDF Pro

Getting Started

  • Click "Add Photos" or drag and drop images
  • Select multiple photos using Ctrl/Cmd + click
  • Photos will appear in a grid with numbered order

Organizing Photos

  • Drag and Drop: Click and drag photos to reorder
  • Auto Sort: Automatically sort by filename
  • Reverse Order: Flip the current order
  • Remove: Click the X button on any photo

PDF Settings

  • Page Size: Choose from A4, Letter, Legal, etc.
  • Orientation: Portrait or Landscape
  • Quality: Balance between file size and image quality
  • Margins: Adjust white space around images

Quick Templates

  • Document: One photo per page, portrait orientation
  • Photo Album: Landscape orientation for photo viewing
  • Collage: Multiple photos per page (coming soon)

Advanced Features

  • Preview: See how your PDF will look before generating
  • Rotate: Click the rotate button on individual photos
  • Fit to Page: Automatically resize images to fit
  • Filename Watermark: Add photo names to PDF pages
