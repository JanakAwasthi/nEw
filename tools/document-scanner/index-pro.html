How to Use Document Scanner Pro

Getting Started

  • Click "Start Camera" to begin scanning documents
  • Or upload images directly using the upload button
  • Drag and drop multiple images onto the camera area

Scanning Features

  • Auto Mode: Automatically detects and captures documents
  • Manual Mode: Click capture button when ready
  • Auto Enhancement: Improves document readability
  • Auto Crop: Detects document edges and crops automatically

Batch Management

  • Scan multiple pages into a single batch
  • Reorder pages by dragging in the sidebar
  • Save batches for later editing
  • Edit individual pages with crop, rotate, and adjustment tools

Export Options

  • PDF: Create searchable PDF documents
  • Images: Export as high-quality JPEG files
  • Word: Generate editable Word documents (OCR)
  • Share: Generate shareable links for your batches
